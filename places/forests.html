Forests

Sinharaja

The Sinharaja Rainforest is a UNESCO World Heritage site and one of the last virgin rainforests on the island. It is home to over 70% of the flora and fauna that is endemic to the island. It was designated a World Biosphere Reserve in 1978.

Kanneliya

Kanneliya or KDN is a forest complex in southern Sri Lanka. The forest complex designated as a biosphere reserve by UNESCO.The KDN complex is the last large remaining rainforest in Sri Lanka other than Sinharaja.

Makandawa

The Makandawa Forest Reserve is one of Sri Lanka’s richest and most unspoiled rainforests. It covers an area of 1155 hectares and lies close to the village of Kithulgala, one of Sri Lanka’s centers for adventure sports.

Galway's park

Galway's Land National Park is a small 27 ha national park, consisting of dense montane forest. It is located within the city limits of Nuwara Eliya in Sri Lanka, approximately 2 km east of the city centre. It was formally declared as a wildlife sanctuary on 27 May 1938.

Ella

Ella is a small town in the Badulla District of Uva Province, Sri Lanka governed by an Urban Council. It is approximately 200 kilometres east of Colombo and is situated at an elevation of 1,041 metres above sea level. The area has a rich bio-diversity.
